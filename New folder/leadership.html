JS Anand

Founder & CEO

A natural leader with a highly successful track record, JS has over 31 years of experience in the E&P industry. He co-founded EnQuest with a vision to deliver world class project management services to the E&P companies.
Prior to turning an entrepreneur, JS was the CEO of Jubilant Energy where he was instrumental in building an attractive asset portfolio, trebling the production of the company & along with partners made the major KG gas discovery. During his brief stint of 18 months, Jubilant saw meteoric rise in its valuation. Prior to taking up the assignment with Jubilant, JS was Cairn Energy’s Asset Manager of its Rajasthan asset and gave India its largest onshore oil discoveries (Mangla, Aishwarya&Bhagyam) in 2004. It was because of his entrepreneurial and drilling project management skills that JS was instrumental in various successful exploration/development campaigns for Cairn Energy in India.
Rich Paces

Advisor

Rich Paces, Advisor-EnQuest PetroSolutions Pvt. Ltd., is a highly accomplished oil and gas professional with over 40 years of global E&P experience across the USA, India (10+ years), Equatorial Guinea, Egypt, Trinidad, China, Russia, Kazakhstan, and Australia. Rich also spent 19 years with Amoco Production Co., building a solid foundation of technical and operational expertise. Known for his extensive executive management experience and a proven track record of successful project execution, Rich has consistently delivered impactful results across diverse geographies and operational challenges in the energy sector.
MP Pant

Senior Vice President - Operations

MP Pant, Senior Vice President - Operations at EnQuest PetroSolutions, is a seasoned oil and gas professional with 40 years of E&P experience across India, Oman, Nigeria, Jordan, Egypt, Chad, and Saudi Arabia. Renowned for his extensive expertise in drilling project management, he has successfully planned and executed numerous drilling campaigns with precision and cost efficiency. His career highlights include serving as Vice President - Operations at EnSearch Petroleum, Drilling Superintendent for Shell Oman, Cairn Energy India, and Enron India, as well as IPM Coordinator for Schlumberger India’s Ravva Project. Starting as a Drilling Superintendent and Rig Manager at Hitech Drilling, a Tata-Sedco Forex JV, MP Pant has consistently demonstrated exceptional project planning and cost management skills, making him a trusted leader in the energy sector.
Tony Beckett

Manager - Well Engineering & Construction

Tony Beckett, Manager - Well Engineering & Construction at EnQuest PetroSolutions, is a veteran in the oil and gas industry with an unparalleled 50+ years of E&P experience across the US, Australia, UK, Africa, India, the Middle East, Egypt, Chad, and Bangladesh. A pioneer in directional drilling, Ultra-deep water drilling, Tony has an exceptional track record in well engineering, exploration geoscience, and geothermal drilling. His leadership roles include General Manager of Operations at Oilex Ltd., General Manager of Drilling for CB-OS/2 & DW KG at Cairn Energy India, and Global Drilling Manager at British Gas. He also served as Drilling Manager for Marathon Australia and Drilling Advisor for HPHT & Deepwater at ONGC. With extensive offshore and onshore experience at Amoco Production Co. and a knack for innovation, including the development of in-house drilling software, Tony continues to drive excellence in upstream operations.
Sunil Vijayan
